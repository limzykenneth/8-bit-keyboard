8-bit Keyboard

Refer to the long sheet on the right to type. Have fun! Or don't.

1 = Button pressed
0 = Button not pressed

Press and hold the corresponding button(s) to choose the character you want.
Press the footpedal to type the character on screen.

To type "a", press and hold the 2nd, 3rd and 8th button from the left then press the foot pedal.

Scan the QR code to bring what you typed with you!
